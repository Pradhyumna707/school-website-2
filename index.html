Delhi PUBLIC SCHOOL-CAMPUS

Delhi Public School is a Central Board of Secondary Education school located in kranti colony RD.,Amravati, India.It also has another branch in Pune, Nagpur, UAE, singapore,banglore,chennai and more. The pre-primary and early years learning programme at DPS is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.
